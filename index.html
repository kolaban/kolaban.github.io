Biography

Oscar is a graduate student in the department of oceanography at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. His research interests include microbial oceanography, data science, and reproducibility. He is a member of the Rappé lab where he works on expanding our understanding of the SAR86 marine bacterial clade.
